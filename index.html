Programming Assessment

Assessment: Build out an interactive simulation that demonstrates what happens to objects in freefall on planet earth.

  • Use earth's gravity.
  • For the atmosphere, provide the following options: air, no air (vacuum).
  • Provide UI elements to start and restart the simulation.
  • Provide UI elements to display elapsed time and velocity.
  • Graphics/animation can be basic/utilitarian -- what's most important is the math model/simulation code.
  • Bonus: provide UI element that displays some/all of the following: forces of gravity, buoyancy, and drag.

Free Fall Velocity Formula

v=v0+gt where:

  • v0 – Initial velocity (measured in m/s or ft/s)
  • t – Fall time (measured in seconds)
  • g – Free fall acceleration (expressed in m/s² or ft/s²)

Free Fall Time Formula

t = 2 h g
  • h– Initial height (measured in m)
  • t – Fall time (measured in seconds)
  • g – Free fall acceleration (expressed in m/s²)
